MCCC Computer Science Map

Computer Information Systems: Computer Science, A.A.S

The Associate of Applied Science (A.A.S) in Computer Science aims to train students for computer programming in an engineering/science environment

Here at MCCC, we have transfer agreements with University of Michigan: Dearborn , Davenport University, and Western Governors University to help students move on to a university to receive a higher level of education in Computer Science.

Required General Education Courses

  • C1: 4 Credits of any Natural Science
  • C2: MATH 171 - Calculus I (4 Credits Hours)
  • C3: ENGL 151 - English Composition I (3 Credit Hours)
  • C4: CIS 130 - Intro to Computer Information Systems (3 Credit Hours)
  • C5: 3 Credits of any Human Experience
  • C6: 3 Credits of any Social Systems
  • Complete list of Competencies
